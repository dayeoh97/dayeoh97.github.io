Overview

The International Student Orientation at York University is a triannual event, which is held to welcome new international students each semester. Due to unusual circumstances this year, York International is planning to hold the Winter 2021 Orientation virtually.

Deliverables

Social Media Promotion

Web Banner

Zoom Background

Welcome Postcard & Stress Ball

Out of the two postcards, one is from York to new students, and the other is blank for students to use. The stress ball is a working concept intended for international students living alone who might feel anxious.

The project’s goal is to design an all-encompassing visual identity for the experience of starting university life. The key to this project is to be creative within the given York University brand guidelines.

Design Challenge

Design a visual identity for the virtual orientation promotional materials and welcome products within brand guidelines.

Solution

Concept

The concept focuses on the fact that this event is held virtually for international students joining from all around the world. The main symbol and a variety of circular textures are tools to illustrate diversity, and a welcoming atmosphere.

Symbol

The symbol is a red globe which represents more than 10,000 international students from 178 countries at York, and the fact that many of them will be joining from their home country virtually.

Texture

Different circular textures demonstrate diversity and the open-mindedness that York International strives for.

Takeaways

There is room for creativity within brand guidelines

The biggest challenge in this project was that despite the constrained brand guidelines, I still had to create something unique. Exploring more organic elements and iterating ideas from the project concept was certainly helpful in achieving this goal.
