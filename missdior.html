Work About Resume

Design for Women

Visual Direction Renewel for an Exhibition

The Miss Dior Exhibition celebrated the brand's rich history and founder, Christian Dior, while unveiling the new perfume. However, the original theme and visual direction failed to capture the elegance of Miss Dior, as they solely focused on promoting the new product. To rectify this, the new exhibition concept, Design for Women, embraces a fresh visual direction inspired by Christian Dior's belief that perfume encompasses more than just a pleasing scent. This concept beautifully reflects his passion for infusing Miss Dior perfumes with gracefulness and elegance.

  • Type: Experience design, Exhibition identity renewal, Packaging, User interface
  • Role: Research, Strategy, Art direction
  • Team: Self-directed passion project. This work is not affiliated with Miss Dior.
