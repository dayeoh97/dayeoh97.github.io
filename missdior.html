Design for Women

Exhibit Visual Direction Renewal


The Miss Dior Exhibition was held to look back at the history of Miss Dior and remember the founder, Christian Dior. The new concept, Design for Women, is inspired by the perspective of Christian Dior that perfume is more than a pleasing scent for a woman.

  • Role
  • Brand Identity Renewal
  • Font Design
  • Package Design
