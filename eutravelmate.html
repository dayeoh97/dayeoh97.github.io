Overview

According to research, solo travel has become a big trend since 2016, and the number of those who go on a trip alone went up by 42% between 2018 and 2019. While some enjoy their time alone, the others are willing to make new friends on the road by staying in hostels and travel together if they find a good match.

Euro Travelmate is an application to connect solo travelers in Europe who are looking for company with similar interests. Through interviewing the target audience— people in their 20s—I found that security was there main concern when using meetup apps in a foreign country.

Design Challenge

Design an app which connects solo travelers in Europe via shared interest in a way that they can feel safe.

Solution

Ensuring Security

Only verified users can make an account. Users must scan their government ID to proceed with registration, the name printed on which will be set as their username.

Providing a safe environment

When users encounter problematic behavior such as spamming, not showing up, or using inappropriate language, they can rest assured that it will be quickly taken care of.

Connect with like-minded travelers

Posts & activities can be set to tailor to the user's current or upcoming location, allowing them to easily find others with the same goals or interests.

Giving users control

In the case a traveler find existing activities or events not to their liking, the app lets them manifest destiny and create their own posts to invite others.

User Persona

Tom Sripantha

  • 20 years old
  • University Student
  • Tech Savvy

Bio

Tom is traveling Europe alone and his first destination is Prague, Czech Republic. He is open-minded, friendly, and loves meeting new people.

Pain Points

  • Afraid to travel Europe alone but couldn’t find a friend to go with him.
  • Used other apps to find friends but did not feel safe with them

Needs

  • Wants to make new friends while traveling.
  • Wishes to find company for going to Jazz Clubs together, and for taking photos.

Kelly Wilson

  • 25 years old
  • Works at Bank of Australia
  • Likes discovering new and trending apps

Bio

Kelly got two weeks of vacation and decided spend it on a trip to France of her dreams. Like Tom, she is also open to meeting new people but is a little more cautious due to bad experiences in the past.

Pain Points

  • Little to no knowledge of France or Europe.
  • Tired of meeting or dealing with troublesome and problematic people.

Needs

  • Company to show her around and give her a good experience of the country.
  • A safe conversational environment.

Takeaways

User testing matters.

It is easy to be stuck with my own perspective when designing an app, as I strongly believed that the profile picture would make it feel like a dating app, but realized that the users feel safer with the profile. It is important to remember that the app is for the users not for myself, and the most users might have different thoughts from a designer.
