Murchie's

Tea Brand Identity Renewal


Murchie's is a Canadian teahouse, proud of its long history and in its British Columbian root. It has been providing its products for the various occasions from local and national events to royal milestones. Rebranding Murchie's aims to create more consistency throughout its visual identity and to represent its brand values more explicitly.

  • Role
  • Brand Identity Renewal
  • Package Design

The Existing Brand

The current visual identity lacks a uniqueness that Murchie's deserves, which fails to represent Murchie's elegance as a heritage brand. The logo seems outdated, and the pattern does not reflect contemporary ideas of luxury.

“Murchie's is a high-end specialty teahouse, proud of its long history since 1894 and its roots in British Columbia, Canada.”

Visual System

The new monogram logo highlights Murchie's prestigious heritage by using a customized Victorian style font with the primary gold colour on black background. The fine lines around the letter M emphasize Murchie's quality products.

Typography
