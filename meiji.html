Meiji X Tokyo 2020 Olympics

Typeface Design for Campagin


Meiji is a large Japanese snack company which exports internationally. This hypothetical campaign will have Meiji sponsoring the Tokyo 2020 Olympics. The campaign's goal is to promote Meiji and increase global interest in the Japanese language by using simple words on Meiji's product packaging.

  • Role
  • Brand Identity Design
  • Marketing Strategies
  • Font Design
  • Package Design

Project Goals

Promote Meiji's brand values

Increase interest in the host country's language

Advertise Meiji's soft, milky products

An appetizing display typeface

The overall round shapes and high x-height make the typeface look cute and friendly.

Its unique curvy lines create a harmonious fluid flow that feels fun.

Heavy weights at the bottom and teardrop shapes are akin to dripping liquid.

Highlight Meiji's fun, tasty products for everyone

Distinctive traits that catch customers' attention

Fluid features that associate with milk
