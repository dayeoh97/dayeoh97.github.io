Experience Exchange

Scholarship Program Identity Design


The Experience Exchange is a new scholarship program by Huge Canada, a global creative agency based in Brooklyn, in partnership with George Brown College. This new partnership aims to improve access to opportunities for underrepresented groups in the design industry. My role in this project was to create the visual direction in all phases, from moodboards to the final deliverables.

  • Role
  • Brand Identity Design
  • Digital Design

Visual System

The Experience Exchange does not simply provide scholarships; it also provides opportunities to exchange ideas and experiences with peers, as well as professionals at Huge, through mentorship. Our team focused on creating a visual identity that illustrates different perceptions and people coming together.
